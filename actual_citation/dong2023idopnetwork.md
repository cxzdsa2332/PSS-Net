## Abstract content [summary — Europe PMC had no record; condensed by Crossref retrieval]

idopNetwork is an R-based cartographic tool to reconstruct informative, dynamic,
omnidirectional and personalized interspecies interaction networks from species
abundance data across geographical locations. It integrates allometric-scaling power
curve fitting, functional clustering, LASSO-based variable selection, quasi-dynamic
ODE solving, and abundance decomposition with network visualization, demonstrated on
gut and plant microbiota datasets.
