## Faithful description (NOT a verbatim quote)

A widely cited review of physics-informed machine learning: integrating physical
principles and governing equations (ODEs/PDEs) with machine-learning models, covering
how prior knowledge is embedded via observational, inductive, and learning biases, and
surveying applications and challenges of data-driven modelling of physical/dynamical
systems.
