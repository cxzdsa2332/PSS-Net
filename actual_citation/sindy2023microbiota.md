- **Venue/Year:** Proc. IEEE EMBC, 2023 (PMID: 38083503)

## Verbatim opening (abstract)

"The gut microbiota is a community of high complexity; its composition changes due to
ecological interactions ..."

## Remainder [summary — condensed by retrieval tool]

Implements SINDy combined with differential neural networks to identify bacterial
interaction parameters from experimental data, reporting less than 2% parametric error
in simulated conditions and sensitivity to drug effects on intraspecies interactions
(≈50% reduction in typical values).

## Supports in intro

B3 — concrete instance of applying SINDy-style nonlinear dynamics identification to
microbial pairwise interactions, i.e. the time-series/derivative-based route that
PSS-Net contrasts with. Establishes that nonlinear data-driven inference is already
being pursued in the microbiome setting.
