## Verbatim opening (abstract)

"It is suggested that a system of chemical substances, called morphogens, reacting
together and diffusing through a tissue, is adequate to account for the main phenomena
of morphogenesis."

## Remainder [summary — condensed by retrieval tool]

Foundational reaction–diffusion theory: shows how instability of an initially
homogeneous chemical system of reacting, diffusing substances can generate organized
spatial patterns, analysed mathematically and illustrated on biological structures.
