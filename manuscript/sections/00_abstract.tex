Reconstructing microbial interaction networks from abundance data is a central
problem in microbial ecology. We propose \pss{}-Net, a sparse additive
nonparametric ODE framework that infers directed interaction networks from
\emph{perturbed steady-state} (\pss{}) experiments. % ... TODO: contributions
